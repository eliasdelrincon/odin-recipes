Pollo en Mole

Mexican Dish!

traditional Mexican dish that showcases the artistry of combining rich, savory flavors with a hint of chocolate undertones. The dish features tender pieces of chicken, typically slow-cooked until succulent, bathed in a velvety mole sauce. The mole, a complex and luscious concoction, is a harmonious blend of various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Dried Chiles
  • Chocolate
